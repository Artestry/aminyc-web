How AMI NYC works

We unify listings from Housing Connect, HPD, HDC, and re-rental sources in one place. Then we match them to your household and route you to the official application.

Step 1

Tell us about your household

Income, household size, and optional details like voucher status. Takes under 2 minutes.

Step 2

See real matches

Eligibility calculated against the same HUD 2024 AMI tables HPD and HDC use. No guesswork.

Step 3

Apply through Housing Connect

One-click handoff to the official application. We are the front door, not a replacement.
