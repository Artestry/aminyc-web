Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 18, 2026

AMI NYC ("the App") is a free, trilingual housing discovery tool that helps New York City residents find affordable housing listings and check their eligibility. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

AMI NYC is designed to be local-first. The vast majority of your data never leaves your device.

Stored on your device only (never transmitted):

  • Household information — income, household size, and eligibility preferences you enter in the Eligibility Checker.
  • Saved listings — listings you bookmark for later review.
  • Applied listings — listings you mark as "applied" after visiting Housing Connect.
  • Document vault files — documents you store in the Document Vault are saved to your device's local storage only. They are not uploaded to any server.
  • Language preference — your chosen language (English, Spanish, or Chinese).

Device permissions the App may request:

  • Location (when in use) — used solely to center the map view on your current location and show nearby listings. Your location is not stored, transmitted, or tracked.
  • Photo library — used solely to let you select documents (photos of IDs, pay stubs, etc.) to store in your private Document Vault. Selected files remain on your device only.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not create user accounts.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels.
  • We do not sell, share, or transmit any personal data to third parties.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or fingerprinting.

3. Third-Party Services

The App contains links to NYC Housing Connect (housingconnect.nyc.gov), the official application portal operated by HPD and HDC. When you tap "Apply," you are redirected to Housing Connect in your device's browser. AMI NYC does not control, access, or receive any data from Housing Connect.

The Map view uses Apple Maps (iOS) and Google Maps (Android). These services have their own privacy policies. AMI NYC does not send your location to any server — the map SDK handles rendering locally on your device.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear All Data" button in the App's Profile/Settings screen, or
  • Uninstalling the App (this removes all locally stored data).

5. Children's Privacy

AMI NYC is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
